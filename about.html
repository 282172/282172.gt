    📈 About Smart-rate

    Smart‑rate was created to provide free, fast, and trustworthy financial calculators for everyone. Our tools run entirely in your browser – no data is sent to our servers, and we don’t track you.

    The currency converter uses multiple public APIs with client‑side caching, so you always get a rate – even when offline or if an API fails. All other calculators (salary, VAT, loan, markup, margin) are pure JavaScript and work instantly.

    We believe financial tools should be accessible to all, without hidden fees or data collection. The name “Smart‑rate” reflects our focus on intelligent, accurate conversions and rates.

    Disclaimer: All tools are for informational purposes only. Please consult a qualified professional before making financial decisions.
